2024

OBJECTIVES

The year 2021 was a crucial starting point for our team, setting the primary goal of laying the groundwork for the development of an innovative and efficient autonomous system. During this year, we focused on exploring the key areas required to build this technology, starting with an in-depth investigation into the sensors that would be essential for accurately collecting environmental data. This involved evaluating multiple technological options, analyzing their capabilities, limitations, and compatibility with our specific needs. Our objective was to identify solutions that ensured the precision and reliability necessary for the system.
At the same time, we dedicated significant effort to analyzing potential algorithms for implementation. This work included researching advanced data processing methods and decision-making models to ensure the system could interpret the collected data and act autonomously. This approach not only established a solid technical foundation but also set a clear direction for future developments. Thus, 2021 was a year devoted to building the pillars of a technology that addresses future challenges and marks the beginning of our commitment to innovation and excellence.

ACHIEVEMENTS

The year 2021 marked a turning point in the development of our autonomous system, solidifying key decisions in fundamental technical areas critical for its implementation. Regarding sensing technologies, an extensive analysis of the available options in the market was conducted, which led to the selection of the ZED 2 camera as the most suitable solution. This camera was chosen due to its ability to deliver high-precision stereo vision, a crucial feature for capturing three-dimensional environmental information. With this technology, the system can better recognize distances and object dimensions in its surroundings, facilitating processes such as mapping and autonomous navigation.
For positioning, the decision to implement an RTK (Real-Time Kinematic) system represented a significant step toward achieving precision and reliability in the project. This system allowed us to achieve centimeter-level accuracy in location data, which is critical for tasks requiring exact positioning, such as trajectory tracking, motion control, and synchronization with other system components. The integration of these technologies not only laid the foundation for a more robust and efficient system but also marked significant progress toward our goal of creating an innovative and reliable autonomous solution.
