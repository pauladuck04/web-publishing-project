OUR HISTORY

The team was born in 2014 with the vision of designing and building a racing car powered by an internal combustion engine. From the beginning, the project was driven by a passion for engineering and a desire to participate in high-level motorsport competitions, demonstrating the technical and creative capacity of the team.

Over the years and with the technological advancement of the automotive sector, the team decided to adapt to new challenges and market trends. In 2021, within the electronics department, some of the members were assigned to an ambitious research project focused on the development of the vehicle of the future: the autonomous car. This strategic shift not only marked a milestone in the team's history, but also reflected its commitment to innovation and sustainability in the automotive world.
