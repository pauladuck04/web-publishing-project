2023

OBJECTIVES

The main objectives of that year were focused on laying the groundwork for the successful development of our autonomous system. A key priority was to begin developing the code for the autonomous system, marking the first steps in creating a fully functional, intelligent vehicle. This involved coding the foundational algorithms that would allow the system to perceive its environment, make decisions, and control the vehicle autonomously.
In addition to the software development, we dedicated significant efforts to designing the chassis that would support the system, ensuring it complied with the regulations required for competition participation. This chassis needed to be both robust and adaptable, providing the necessary framework to integrate all the system's components while meeting the specific criteria set by the competition organizers.
Furthermore, we developed a small remote control (RC) vehicle to test the code in real-world conditions. This prototype allowed us to simulate the autonomous system's performance in a controlled environment before transitioning to a full-scale model. The RC vehicle served as an essential testing tool, enabling us to debug and refine the code and ensure that the system would function correctly in real-world scenarios. These objectives laid the foundation for future advancements and set the team on a path toward successfully competing in upcoming events.

ACHIEVEMENTS

This season, our team achieved a significant milestone by participating in our first competition within the AI department. Despite facing several challenges, we were able to secure 13th place in the overall ranking, which was a notable accomplishment given the circumstances. Although we were unable to complete any of the dynamic tests during the competition, the experience proved invaluable in identifying areas for improvement and gaining a deeper understanding of the competition's technical demands.
The fact that we achieved a solid position despite not completing the dynamic challenges highlights the progress we made in terms of system development, team coordination, and problem-solving. It also reinforced the importance of thorough preparation and testing in real-world conditions. This participation marked an important learning opportunity, setting the stage for future improvements and better performance in upcoming competitions. The insights gained from this experience will drive our efforts as we continue to refine our autonomous system and work toward our long-term goals.
