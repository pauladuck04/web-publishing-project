Top 7 en FSUK-AI 2024

Published on 26 of July, 2024

The UVigo Motorsport Driverless team took part last week in Formula Student UK. This competition brings together arious university teams from around the world who strive to achieve the best results. This year, after being their second season attending Formula Student UK AI, UVigo Motorsport's Driverless made their mark by achieving a seventh place and a total of 364.8 points in the Overall.

This 2023/2024 season, the level of competition increased significantly compared to last year so fighting for every position became more important than ever. It is worth mentioning the Formula Student Simracing championship in which we came fifth out of 32 participants.

This year was the first time in the history of Driverless that they were able to pass one of the Dynamic tests: the Track Drive, which consists of driving ten laps on a random circuit. We received a total of 100 points for completing all ten laps. The other dynamic tests: the Sprint, Skidpad and Acceleration were not passed due to various problems with the standalone software. The completion of this test was a source of pride for the whole team as it is very difficult to complete the dynamic tests due to the complexity of the autonomous system, something that very few teams achieve.

On the other hand, in the Static tests we highlight the Business Plan Presentation, reaching a total of 74 points and doubling the score with respect to the previous year, reaching a meritorious P9. In addition, in Simulation we received a total of 48.8 points. On the other hand, in ADR and Real World AI we improved our score with respect to last year with 92 and 50 points respectively in these tests.

One of our main objectives was to complete some of the dynamic tests in the competition and we more than achieved this thanks to TrackDrive.

It has been a competition full of many different emotions and a lot of hard work. The level of knowledge and work was noticeable in the different teams that participated, being able to learn a lot from them, being a season of which we can not feel more proud for all the success achieved. From this competition we take with us the work of each one of us accompanied by all the obstacles overcome and the emotion and pride of our autonomous system.
