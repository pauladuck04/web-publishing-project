UvigoMotorsport at CESGA

Published on 18 of March, 2024

On 6 March, the UVigo Motorsport team had the privilege of visiting the facilities of the Galician Supercomputing Centre (CESGA), as part of its collaboration with the team. CESGA is recognised as the most important supercomputing centre in Galicia, standing out in the field of high-performance computing and quantum technologies.

On the day of our visit, we were cordially received by the Director of CESGA, Lois Orosa Nogueira, with whom the Team Leader of UVigo Motorsport, Denis Pallarés Roque, formalised the collaboration agreement, as we had done in previous seasons.

In addition, we had the kindness of Fernando Bouzas Sierra, who guided us through all the CESGA facilities, which were extremely interesting. One of the highlights was a very peculiar quantum computer, of which few of us have had the opportunity to see it, as it was undergoing maintenance work.

During our visit, Pablo Dorrío Vázquez and Sergio Enrique Aramburu García representing autonomous, together with the head of Organisation and Marketing Óscar Rodríguez Fernández had the opportunity to explore CESGA's facilities in detail and to admire its supercomputer, the Finisterrae III, installed in 2022. With this supercomputer we have boosted the training of complex neural networks, using its parallel calculation capacity to process large data sets and thus adjust many parameters. This allows us, using deep-learning techniques, to accurately model a perception of the environment independent of the environmental context, which is an advantage when attending Formula Student competitions.
