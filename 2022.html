2022

OBJECTIVES

The main objective was to begin investigating the content of static tests, understanding the dynamics of the competition, and starting the development of a prototype for the structure of the autonomous system. During this year, we focused on gaining a deep understanding of the types of static tests that would be necessary to assess the performance and reliability of the system. This included exploring the technical requirements and conditions under which the system would be evaluated.
In parallel, we worked on analyzing how the competition would function, ensuring that our system was designed to meet the specific challenges and standards required. This involved studying the rules, constraints, and expectations of the competition to align our objectives accordingly.
Additionally, we started developing a prototype that would lay the groundwork for the overall structure of the autonomous system. This early prototype aimed to define the key components and how they would integrate, providing a clear vision for the final system's architecture. This work was essential in shaping the future direction of the project and ensuring that the team was prepared for the next stages of development.

ACHIEVEMENTS

Our team achieved significant milestones that have laid a strong foundation for the future of our autonomous vehicle project. One of the key achievements was the establishment of the final structure for the first prototype of our autonomous car. This involved designing and integrating the core components that would define the overall architecture, ensuring a cohesive and functional system. The prototype served as a crucial step in visualizing the complete system and refining the design to meet the performance requirements for autonomous operation.
Another important achievement was the completion of a comprehensive study on the FSUK-AI competition. This research was vital for understanding the competition's technical challenges, rules, and performance expectations. By thoroughly analyzing the competition framework, we were able to tailor our development process to align with the specific demands of future events. This preparation not only ensured we were ready for the upcoming challenges but also helped us identify areas for improvement and optimization in our system, setting the stage for future success in the competitive arena.
