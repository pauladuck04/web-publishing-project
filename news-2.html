Top 10 en FSUK 2023

Published on 25 of July, 2023

Throughout last week, the team of the University of Vigo competed against teams from around the world in a very special edition of the Formula Student United Kingdom (FSUK), its 25th anniversary.

For this special occasion, UVigo Motorsport was presented to the event with 2 teams, the traditional combustion team, and an important novelty, for the first time, the Autonomous Department participated in a competition of autonomous cars, FSUK Artificial Intelligence (FSUK AI).

After arriving at the circuit last Tuesday 18 July 2023, the 44 team members in attendance started work, to get everything ready before starting the next day's inspections.

On behalf of the AI team, the autonomous system was able to pass all the safety inspections and technical tests, thus being able to participate in all the dynamic tests scheduled for the prototype.

As for the UM23, all the technical and safety inspections were passed on the first day of competition without any major mishaps. On the following day, the three technical tests required for the combustion vehicles were completed, as well as those for the 4 drivers. Up to this point, UVigo Motorsport was the 8th team to pass these inspections in record time for us, which allowed the team to spend time fine-tuning the car for the next day's tests.

Once the car was ready, and all the tests and inspections had been passed, the only thing left to do was to run the car. So, over the course of the weekend, the UM23 was able to run every single dynamic test, which we had only achieved twice before in the team's history.

On Saturday morning, the Skidpad and Acceleration were successfully completed, achieving 6th and 11th place respectively, while in the afternoon, the Sprint test was successfully completed, obtaining another 6th place. Finishing Saturday with the first 3 dynamic tests successfully passed, only the most demanding test of the Formula Student, the Endurance, was left for Sunday.

When it was time to enter the race, the order of entry was reversed to the ranking of the previous day's sprint times, so it was not until after midday that the participation window for the UM23 was opened. Unfortunately, a sensor failure made it impossible to finish the race, and although the problem was easily solved, there was no option to try again.

All these results, added to the 6th place in the Engineering Design Report, the 4th in Lap Time Simulation, and the points obtained in the rest of the static tests, gave the team a total score of 344.4 points, which is the highest score obtained by the team in a competition and which translates into an incredible 10th position in the overall ranking of the competition, which includes all the teams, both internal combustion and electric, which adds up to more than 60 teams, so it is a result of which we are very proud.

As a final touch, the team attended the farewell gala and awards ceremony on Sunday evening, with the intention of supporting and congratulating the other teams. But they left in a very different way to the way they came in. Everyone in the team was surprised when, at one point during the ceremony, they heard "car number 14, UVigo Motorsport" called to the stage to present us with the Best Newcomer 2023 trophy, which is awarded to the team with the best results among those competing in FSUK for the first time.

We are not only proud, but euphoric about the results. They are at the level of our best expectations, and we can only consider them a resounding success and the greatest possible reward for our months of effort and dedication.
